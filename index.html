Medicare context, in plain English
Original Medicare A & B does NOT cover routine medical care abroad.

Per your call today, Medicare will only reimburse foreign medical bills in narrow exceptions, and does not pay for any medical evacuation. The job of trip insurance here is twofold: (1) cover medical care you receive abroad while you're abroad, and (2) get you flown home if anything serious happens — at which point your Medicare A & B + Anthem will pay normally for U.S. care.

That makes medical evacuation back to the U.S. the single most important coverage to confirm in any plan you pick.
